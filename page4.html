Rule Book

1. Game Duration

  • Four quarters, each lasting 12 minutes
  • Overtime periods of 5 minutes if the score is tied

2. Scoring

  • Field Goal: 2 points (3 points if shot from beyond the arc)
  • Free Throw: 1 point
  • Team with the most points at the end of the game wins

3. Fouls

  • Personal fouls: Illegal physical contact with an opponent
  • Technical fouls: Unsportsmanlike conduct or violations of game rules
  • Players are disqualified after 6 personal fouls

4. Violations

  • Traveling: Taking too many steps without dribbling
  • Double Dribble: Dribbling, stopping, and then dribbling again
  • Shot Clock: Team must attempt a shot within 24 seconds

5. Out of Bounds

  • Ball is out of bounds when it touches the floor or any object outside the court lines
  • Turnover occurs if a player steps out of bounds while holding the ball

6. Substitutions

  • Teams can substitute players during stoppages in play
  • Substitutions must be made at the scorer's table

7. Overtime Rules

  • If the score is tied at the end of regulation, overtime periods are played
  • Each team gets one timeout per overtime period

8. Instant Replay

  • Used to review certain calls, such as buzzer-beaters or flagrant fouls
  • Coaches can challenge one call per game

9. Game Officials

  • Game is officiated by a crew of referees and umpires
  • Officials have the authority to enforce rules and maintain order on the court

10. Sportsmanship

  • Players and coaches are expected to show respect to officials, opponents, and the game itself
  • Unsportsmanlike conduct may result in technical fouls or ejections

11. Dimensions

  • Court Size: 28 meters (length) x 15 meters (width)
  • Free-Throw Line: 4.6 meters from the backboard
  • Three-Point Line: 6.75 meters from the basket (international), 7.24 meters (NBA)
  • Basket Height: 3.05 meters (10 feet) above the floor
  • Backboard Size: 1.8 meters (width) x 1.05 meters (height)
  • Ring Diameter: 45 centimeters (18 inches)

12. Equipment

  • Basketball: Size 7 (29.5 inches in circumference)
  • Uniforms: Teams must wear matching jerseys and shorts
  • Shoes: Players must wear appropriate basketball shoes with non-marking soles
